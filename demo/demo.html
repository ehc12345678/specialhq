Welcome

Welcome to SpecialHQ, your special needs headquarters. We have put this website together with the goal of improving the lives of special need parents and guardians, child caregivers, and providers. We recognize that caring for someone with special needs can be difficult sometimes, and that some of that care requires expertise that must come from a professional. We also know that a parent of a recently diagnosed special needs child may be asking themselves: "now what?", "what do I do from here?" and "who can help me?". SpecialHQ is here to provide answers
